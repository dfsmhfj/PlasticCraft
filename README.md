## What does this Mod add?

This Mod introduces a wide variety of content centered around a new material: **Plastic**.

The additions include:
* Many **plastic-related items, blocks, and equipment**.
* **Rubber Trees** and **tools for obtaining rubber**, as plastic is crafted from rubber.

Come and enjoy the contrast between the **hardness of plastic** and the **softness of rubber!**
